📞 Corsair Airlines Customer Service – Full Direct Connect Guide

Need to get in touch with Corsair Airlines? Our customer service is accessible through multiple direct channels based on your location and preference. This guide will help you reach the right team quickly and easily. 🌍📲

📱 Phone Contact Details

Call us for real-time support on bookings, check-ins, baggage, and more:

  • 🇫🇷 From France: Dial 3917 (€0.35/min from landlines)
  • 🌍 International: +1-888-829-1117
  • 🇺🇸 North America: +1 888-657-8380
  • 🏢 Local offices: New York & Los Angeles both use +1 888-657-8380

📧 Email Support

If you prefer to communicate via email, send your inquiry to:

corsair.res@apg-ga.ca

Include the following for quicker assistance:

  • 👤 Full name
  • 📘 Booking reference
  • 🛫 Flight details
  • ✏️ A clear explanation of the issue

⏰ Operating Hours

  • 🇫🇷 France: Daily from 7:00 AM – 11:00 PM (local time)
  • 🇺🇸 North America: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:30 PM (local time)

🛬 On-Site Support at Airports

For urgent issues like flight changes, baggage claims, or check-in help, visit a Corsair service desk at the airport. 🛄💼

Have your booking reference and ID ready to speed up the process. 🧾🆔
